HOW IT WORKS

Enrolling in gbSTEM

  • All sessions are free of charge and will be conducted remotely via Zoom. To sign up, fill out this form. All students are expected to be able to commit to at least two 45-60 minute long sessions a week; please do not register your child if you will be unable to fulfill the weekly time commitment. It is also recommended that students spend time outside of lessons to work on the material.
  • Please understand that once a student enrolls in a course, they will be unable to switch levels. With this in mind, kindly make sure that your child fully understands the prerequisites for the course they want to take. You can find a list of Computer Science prerequisites here and a list of math prerequisites here.
  • If you have any questions about our program, please read the FAQ before emailing us with inquiries.
  • To meet the founders and instructors, check out the Our Team tab.
