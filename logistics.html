Registering

Before filling out the registration form, all students must take the placement test (located in the "classes" tab of this website). After completing the test, the test will automatically provide you with course recommendations and a code to copy and paste into the registration form. This code will allow us to place students with similar ability in the same class.

The registration form must be submitted by March 7th, 2022. No late submissions will be accepted. We will contact you shortly after the deadline with more logistics about the semester.

Classes

Classes will either be conducted remotely via Zoom. All classes will meet twice a week (available time slots can be found on the registration form) for 1 hour per session. Please do not register your child if you will be unable to fulfill the weekly time commitment - if you register for 3 classes (a CS class, math class, and an engineering class), the time commitment will be 6 hours a week. We also recommend students to study the material outside of class.

Please understand that once a student enrolls in a course, they will be unable to switch levels. With this in mind, kindly make sure that your child fully understands the prerequisites for the course they want to take. Specifically, we do not recommend enrolling in a course higher than what was recommended in the placement test.
