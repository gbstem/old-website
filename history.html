HISTORY

gbSTEM was founded during Spring of 2020. During this first semester, gbSTEM offered only one official course: Python. In addition, it offered free tutoring for Scratch and mathematics. At the end of that semester, the CS track held a virtual final event where the Python and Scratch students presented their final projects to each other.
