PLACEMENT QUIZ

All students are required to take the following placement test before registering for gbSTEM. This test will take less than 30 minutes to complete. The results of this test will help the student make the right course selection and help us put students of similar ability in the same class.

Before starting the test, the student should be seated in a quiet place in front of a laptop or desktop computer. They should use the bathroom prior to taking this test. No breaks are allowed during this session as there is a time limit for each question. The student should also have next to them a piece of paper and pencil for scratch work. No calculator is allowed, and the student may receive no help from anyone during the test. To start the test, click the start button below.
