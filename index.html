FREE COMPUTER SCIENCE AND MATH COURSES

The Greater Boston STEM Program (gbSTEM) delivers free introductory computer science and math courses to elementary and middle school students. gbSTEM will run from March 6th to June 19th, 2021. Registrations for the gbSTEM Program will close on February 23, 2021.

HOW IT WORKS

All sessions are free and will be conducted remotely via Zoom in groups of less than 6 students. Each class will meet for two 45-60 minute sessions every week. We emphasize creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration in order to foster a welcoming environment where students can form lasting friendships.

OUR MISSION

Computer science is one of the fastest-growing industries in the country, and many employers love to hire applicants with coding experience. Mathematics forms the basis of our world, and reasoning skills are needed all throughout life. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to build strong foundational skills in computer science and math, no matter what their background or location is.
