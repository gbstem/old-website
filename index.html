HOW IT WORKS

All sessions are free, and will be conducted remotely via Zoom, either 1:1 or in groups of less than 5 students. To sign up, fill out this form. All students are expected to be able to commit to at least two 45-60 minute long sessions a week. It is also recommended that students spend time outside of lessons to work on the material. Class times will vary by student and will be arranged with the student's instructor, taking into account what works best for the instructors and students.

If you have any questions about our program, please read the FAQ before emailing us with inquiries.

To meet the founders and instructors, check out the Our Team tab.



Computer science is one of the fastest-growing industries in the country, and many employers

love to hire applicants with coding experience.

Mathematics forms the basis of our world, and reasoning skills are needed all throughout life.

However, according to this national poll, only about 40% of K-12 schools offer computer science courses.

Additionally, this Pew Research poll finds that 52% of Americans believe students don't pursue math and science degrees because they think the subjects are too hard.

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to build strong foundational skills in math and

computer science, no matter what their background or their location.
