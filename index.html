HOW IT WORKS

All sessions are free, and will be conducted remotely via Zoom, either 1:1 or in groups of less than 5 students. To sign up, fill out this form. All students are expected to be able to commit to at least two 45-60 minute long sessions a week. It is also recommended that students spend time outside of lessons to work on the material. Class times will vary by student and will be arranged with the student's instructor, taking into account what works best for the instructors and students.

If you have any questions about our program, please read the FAQ before emailing us with inquiries.

To meet the founders and instructors, check out the Our Team tab. The program philosophy can be accessed through the Our Mission tab.
