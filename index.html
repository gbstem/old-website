The Greater Boston STEM Program (gbSTEM) delivers free introductory computer science and math courses to elementary and middle school students. The gbSTEM fall 2021 semester will run from September 26th to December 18th, 2021. Registrations for gbSTEM will close on September 17th.

How it works

All sessions are free and will be conducted remotely via Zoom in groups of about 5 students. Each class will meet for two 60 minute sessions every week. We emphasize creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration in order to foster a welcoming environment where students can form lasting friendships.
