Elena Su

Brookline High School '21

DIRECTOR OF MATHEMATICS TRACK

Elena has loved math ever since her childhood. She is one of the captains of the math team at her high school, and she participates in competitions such as the AMC (American Math Competition) and the AIME (American Invitational Mathematics Exam), a three-hour examination that she has qualified for three times. In her freshman year, she made the Distinguished Honor Roll on the AMC 10, placing her at the top 1% of competitors. She has also competed at the Harvard-MIT Math Tournament and at the Math Prize for Girls at MIT. She has attended Canada/USA Mathcamp and participated in the MIT PRIMES Circle reading group.

Recently, Elena's statistics research paper was accepted to the International Young Researcher's Conference in Cambridge. She is currently taking Multivariable Calculus. In her spare time, she enjoys creative writing, reporting, and drawing.
